What is lancexhudson.eth.limo ?

This is my Web3 domain name. Inspired by the DNS protocol that takes IP addresses and makes them more digestible, the Ethereum Name Service (ENS) converts Ethereum wallet addresses into human-readable names. The one key difference between the two protocols is that with a Web3 .eth domain, the permissionless exchange of value is now possible with blockchain technology.

lancexhudson.eth is my ethereum address in a human readable form. Without it, my web3 address would like something like... 0x4fEfcc5a3B0701d6Ee073003058d1c66E1d54A1d and that is just nasty.
